Wild Geese

Wild Geese is a mixed-media animation based on the poem Wild Geese by Mary Oliver. The process of creating these visuals involved filming video, exporting individual frames of the video, making physical alterations by hand to each frame, and then scanning these frames to be put into a video sequence.

Publication

The Wild Geese publication below is a digital replication of a physical printed book. This publication includes my interpertation of the Wild Geese poem by Mary Oliver, a transcribed interview that dives into the process of creating the visual piece, and some process work/scanned video frames from my visual piece.
