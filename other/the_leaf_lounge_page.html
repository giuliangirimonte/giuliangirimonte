The Leaf Lounge

The Leaf Lounge is an interactive web design project focused on helping users easily care for their plants. The site features a searchable plant library with detailed care information, along with a calendar tool that allows users to set recurring reminders on their device for watering and maintenance. Paired with a "lounge" focused on plant-care tools and an ambient-sound collection, the website encourages a more mindful approach to plant care.
